MedFamily Reminder respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the MedFamily Reminder mobile application handles user information.

About the App

MedFamily Reminder is a simple medicine reminder app designed to help users manage daily medication routines for themselves and their family members.

Information We Collect

MedFamily Reminder does not require account registration. The app does not collect personal information on a remote server.

The information entered into the app may include:

  • family member names or labels
  • medication names
  • dose information
  • reminder times
  • instructions entered by the user
  • local medication history
  • language and appearance settings

How We Use Information

The information entered into the app is used only to provide the core app functionality:

  • creating and managing family members
  • creating medication reminders
  • showing daily medication tasks
  • marking medication as taken or skipped
  • displaying local history
  • saving language and interface preferences

Data Storage

All user data is stored locally on the user’s device using local app storage.

MedFamily Reminder does not sell user data. MedFamily Reminder does not share personal data with third parties.

Internet Access

The current version of MedFamily Reminder does not require an online account for its basic functionality.

If future versions add cloud backup, online sync, or other network-based features, this Privacy Policy may be updated.

Analytics and Advertising

The current version of MedFamily Reminder does not use advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or tracking tools.

Children’s Privacy

MedFamily Reminder is not specifically directed to children under 13. If the app is used by families to manage reminders for children, the data remains under the control of the device owner.

MedFamily Reminder is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always follow the instructions of a qualified healthcare professional.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
